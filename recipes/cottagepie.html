Cottage Pie

Description

Shepherd's pie, cottage pie, or in its French version hachis Parmentier is a savoury dish of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato and baked.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • All-purpose flour
  • Italian seasoning
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Parsley
  • Beef broth
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper
  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Take a pie
  2. Add some cottage
  3. Maybe a shepherd too if you're up for it
